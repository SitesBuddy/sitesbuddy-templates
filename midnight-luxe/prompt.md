# Midnight Luxe — Template Context

## Page Type
Premium dark editorial sales/landing page.

## Design System
- **Palette**: Obsidian (#0D0D12), Champagne (#C9A84C), Ivory (#FAF8F5)
- **Fonts**: Playfair Display (serif headings), Inter (body), JetBrains Mono (accents)
- **Style**: Fixed navbar, noise texture overlays, GSAP scroll animations, premium feel
- **Tone**: Exclusive, sophisticated, private members' club

## Section Structure
1. **Hero** — Full-height dark backdrop + champagne headline + serif subhead + CTA button
2. **Problem** — "Sound familiar?" with 3 pain-point bullets
3. **Solution / Features** — 3 feature cards with icons
4. **Social Proof** — 2–3 testimonials with name and role
5. **CTA Block** — Final push with repeated CTA
6. **Footer** — Brand name + tagline + legal

## Suggested First Edits
- Change the headline and subheadline to match your business
- Replace the testimonial names and quotes with real client feedback
- Update the CTA button text and adjust the champagne accent color
