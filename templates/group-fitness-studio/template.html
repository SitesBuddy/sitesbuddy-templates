Good to know

First-class questions.

Do I need to be fit before I start?

No. Tell the instructor it is your first class and they will help you choose a pace that feels useful.

Can I see prices before joining?

Yes. Drop-ins are €14, a 5-class pack is €59, and monthly memberships start at €54.

What if I need to cancel?

Cancel up to four hours before class and the credit returns to your account.

Is the studio accessible?

The entrance, main studio, and accessible changing room are step-free. Contact us if you want help planning your visit.
