01 · Teaching approach

Students learn more when the room belongs to their thinking.

My working thesis
Structure should make room for curiosity, disagreement, and revision without making expectations vague.
01

Begin with a problem

Units open with a question worth arguing about, then build the knowledge students need to answer it responsibly.

02

Make thinking visible

Students annotate sources, compare claims, and explain how evidence changed their first interpretation.

03

Assess the whole process

Feedback covers preparation, discussion, drafting, reflection, and the final public-facing work.
