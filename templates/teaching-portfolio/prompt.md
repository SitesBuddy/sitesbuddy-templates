# Teaching Portfolio

Build a concise professional portfolio for a secondary-school teacher applying for roles or presenting curriculum work.

The page should help a hiring committee understand the educator's personality, teaching philosophy, classroom practice, selected evidence, experience, and credentials without turning the website into a document archive.

Required sections:

- Fixed navigation with portfolio CTA
- Editorial teacher portrait and clear subject/grade focus
- Subtle marquee summarizing the teaching philosophy
- Teaching thesis and three working principles
- Three detailed but concise unit case studies
- Supervisor feedback
- Teaching experience and professional learning
- Contact CTA suitable for hiring or collaboration

Use an understated editorial style, serious readable typography, student-safe examples, local imagery, SitesBuddy structural hooks, and responsive navigation.
