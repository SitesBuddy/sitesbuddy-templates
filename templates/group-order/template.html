Simple process

How group ordering works

Five steps, no faff. You'll be in your first order within minutes of signing up.

1
👋
Sign up for free
Create an account with your name, address, and postcode. No payment needed upfront. Takes about 90 seconds.
2
🔍
Browse open orders
See what's currently being organised by category, producer, or delivery area. Click any open order to see full details and pricing.
3
✅
Join and add your share
Tell us how much you want. We don't charge your card until the group minimum is hit. If we don't fill the order, you're never charged.
4
📦
We place the order
Once minimum numbers are reached, we place the bulk order with the producer and confirm your delivery date and pickup point.
5
🎉
Collect or get delivered
Pick up from your local drop point (usually within 1km), or opt into home delivery if your address is on the route. That's it!
Why it works

Shop like a wholesale buyer,
even as a household

💶
Wholesale prices
Producers give us bulk discounts when we order in volume. That saving goes directly to you — we only add a small coordination fee.
🌿
Less waste, less packaging
Bulk deliveries mean fewer trips, fewer boxes, and less plastic than individual orders. Better for your wallet, better for the planet.
🤝
Support local producers
Most of our suppliers are small businesses. Your group order may represent 20–30% of their weekly revenue. That matters to them.
🛡️
No charge if order doesn't fill
We only charge your card when we've hit the minimum needed to place the order. If we can't fill it, you're not charged — ever.
