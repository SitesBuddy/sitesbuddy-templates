Why it matters

Good reasons to buy local

It's not just a nice feeling. There are real, measurable effects when you choose local over imported.

💶
Money stays local
Studies show that for every €10 spent locally, around €6.50 recirculates in the local economy. National chains send it elsewhere.
🌍
Lower food miles
A jar of honey from three miles away has a very different carbon footprint from one shipped from Argentina. It's that simple.
👐
Real people, not brands
Behind every product here is someone who grew it, baked it, or made it by hand. That matters — in quality and in accountability.
🔗
Supply resilience
When global supply chains stutter — and they do — local producers can keep delivering. No containers, no delays, no shortages.
