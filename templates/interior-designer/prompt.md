# Interior Designer

Build an image-first, editorial website for an independent residential interior design studio. The visitor is planning a meaningful home project and needs enough proof, taste, and process clarity to start an enquiry. Lead with a distinctive finished interior, then help the visitor identify their project type: room reset, renovation, or full-home furnishing. Use a warm stone, clay, moss, and ink palette; calm human copy; material-led photography; and a clear email enquiry action. The design should feel refined and personal without being ornate or overly luxurious.
