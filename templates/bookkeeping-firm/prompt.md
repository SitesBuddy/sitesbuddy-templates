# Bookkeeping Firm

Build a warm, plain-speaking website for a small-business bookkeeping firm. The visitor is a busy owner who wants their finances organised and needs a low-pressure discovery call before committing. Turn the home page into a clear route from “my books are behind or unclear” to a 20-minute conversation. Explain monthly bookkeeping, catch-up work, and owner-ready reporting in concrete language. Use a soft sage, paper, tan, and ink palette; imagery of real collaborative work; no exaggerated financial claims; and straightforward calls to action such as “Book a 20-minute call.”
