Your first visit

Arrive curious. Leave knowing what suits you.

Come ten minutes early and meet your teacher at reception. We’ll show you the room, ask about anything we should know, and help set up your equipment.

  • Wear what lets you breatheFitted or loose is fine. Grip socks are useful for reformer classes but not required for your first visit.
  • Tell us what your body needsInjuries, pregnancy, and recent surgery matter. Share what feels relevant before class begins.
  • Stay after if you have questionsYour teacher can suggest the next class based on how the session felt.

Good to know

First-visit questions.

Can complete beginners join reformer Pilates?

Yes. Choose Reformer Foundations. The class begins with the machine setup and uses a slower pace.

What if I have back pain or an injury?

Contact us before booking. We may suggest a smaller class, private session, or clearance from your healthcare professional.

Do I need to bring a mat?

No. Mats, blocks, straps, bolsters, and other studio props are included.

What does a regular membership cost?

Four classes per month start at €64, eight classes at €112, and unlimited mat and yoga classes at €119.
