Ask about placement

Before the first class

Parent questions, answered.

What should my child wear to the trial?

Comfortable clothes, tied-back hair, and bare feet or clean socks are enough. Please wait before buying dancewear or shoes.

Can parents watch?

Parents may watch the first trial for younger dancers. We also hold scheduled observation weeks during each term.

How do you choose the right level?

Age is the starting point. The teacher will suggest the best placement after seeing the dancer move and learn in class.

Is performance compulsory?

No. The annual studio sharing is optional, and costume costs are explained before families decide.
