# Local Essentials — Template Customisation Guide

This is a neighbourhood shop / convenience store template. It prioritises key practical info (hours, location, phone) and communicates a trustworthy, community-rooted feel.

## Key things to customise

**Shop identity**
- Replace "Corner & Co." with the actual shop name in the nav, hero, footer, and top strip
- Update the tagline ("Your neighbourhood essential") to suit the shop's angle
- Change the address in the top strip, hero card, and contact section

**Hours & contact**
- Update Monday–Saturday and Sunday hours in the top strip, hero card, and footer
- Replace phone number (currently 01 000 0000) in all `tel:` links
- Update the email address in the contact section

**Departments grid**
- Replace or remove any of the 8 department cards to match what the shop actually stocks
- Change the emoji icons to match the categories
- Update descriptions to reflect what's unique about this shop's range

**Weekly specials**
- Replace all 4 special offer cards with real current deals
- Update product names, descriptions, old price, new price, and discount percentage badge

**About section**
- Update the founding year (currently 2004)
- Replace the text to reflect the real shop history and ownership
- The trust items (open 7 days, family-owned, etc.) should be edited to be accurate

**Contact section**
- Replace address and directions text
- The map placeholder (🗺️ emoji on a coloured background) can be replaced with a real embedded Google Maps iframe

## What this template works for
- Village convenience stores
- Neighbourhood supermarkets or off-licences
- Petrol station shops
- Pharmacy-attached convenience stores
- Any small retail outlet that benefits from prominent hours/location info
