Our story

Part of this street
for over 20 years.

Corner & Co. opened in 2004 as a small convenience store. Since then we've grown to carry over 2,000 products while keeping the feel of a local shop where staff know your name.

We believe in fair prices, fresh stock, and being there when the bigger shops are closed. That's what a neighbourhood shop is for.
