Our story
"We got tired of eating food that had been on a truck for three days. So we started growing our own — and then our neighbours wanted some too."

Greenfield Farm started as a hobby plot in 2014. Today we grow on 4 acres, raise a small flock of hens, and sell directly to families in the area every Saturday. No middlemen, no cold storage, no compromise on quality.

We grow using traditional and sustainable methods — no synthetic pesticides, no artificial fertilisers. Just good soil, good seed, and good timing.

— Tom & Aoife, Greenfield Farm
