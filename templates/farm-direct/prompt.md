# Farm Direct — Template Customisation Guide

This is a direct farm-to-customer sales page for a farm, smallholding, or market garden.

## Key things to customise

**Farm identity**
- Replace "Thornfield Farm" with the actual farm name throughout
- Update the tagline in the nav and hero to reflect what the farm grows/sells
- Change the hero subheading to match the main product (e.g. "eggs", "veg boxes", "lamb", "dairy")

**Products**
- The harvest grid shows 6 product cards — replace the emoji, product name, price, and description
- Badges (Fresh / Limited / Seasonal) should reflect real stock status
- Remove or add cards as needed — the grid is CSS auto-fill and will reflow

**How it works**
- The 3 steps describe an order and pickup process — update the day names, times, and any instructions specific to this farm

**Info band**
- Update the pickup location, opening hours, phone number, and email
- If delivery is available (not just pickup), change "Saturday Pickup" to reflect that

**About section**
- Replace the farmer quote with a real one or a short personal story
- The tone should be warm and personal — this is a high-trust sale

**Reviews**
- Replace with real customer quotes if available, or remove the section

**Colours**
- `--green: #1d3a28` — deep forest green (nav, hero, CTA)
- `--amber: #c8711a` — warm amber (accents, hover states)
- `--cream: #f5f0e5` — off-white background

## What this template works for
- CSA (community supported agriculture) subscription boxes
- Market garden or smallholding selling direct
- Egg or dairy farm with regular pickup
- Seasonal produce sales (e.g. strawberry season, Christmas turkeys)
