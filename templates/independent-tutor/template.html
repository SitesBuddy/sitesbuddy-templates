How it works

A useful first session, not a sales call.

We begin with the learner’s current work and one real difficulty. The next steps come from what we notice together.

Check availability
01

Tell me the context

Share the subject, year level, current concern, and any useful school material. A short note is enough.

02

Try one focused session

We work on a real task, notice the sticking point, and test an explanation or strategy that fits the learner.

03

Receive a clear update

Families get a brief note covering what we practised, what improved, and what to do before the next session.

04

Choose what happens next

Continue weekly, book a short exam block, or stop after the immediate need is resolved. There is no long contract.

Parents stay informed

Know what happened after the session.

Useful tutoring does not disappear behind a closed door. Each update is short enough to read and specific enough to support the learner without turning home into another classroom.

Sample session note

Algebra · Week 3

55 min
What we worked on
Translating word problems into two-step equations.
What clicked
Sam began checking the meaning of each answer, not only the calculation.
Before next time
Complete questions 2, 4, and 6. Stop after ten focused minutes if frustration rises.
