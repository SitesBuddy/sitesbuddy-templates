# Independent Tutor

Build a warm, parent-facing website for an independent secondary maths tutor and learning-support teacher.

The page should help a parent understand who the tutor supports, how sessions work, what feedback the family receives, what it costs, and how to request a first session.

Required sections:

- Fixed navigation with one enquiry CTA
- Trust-led tutoring hero with local photography
- Distinct service-summary marquee
- Three support pathways based on current needs
- Four-step first-session process
- Sample parent session note
- Transparent session and short-block pricing
- Parent FAQ accordion
- Focused enquiry CTA

Use calm family-home photography, a warm moss and clay palette, human copy, accessible interactions, SitesBuddy structural hooks, and clear responsive behavior.
