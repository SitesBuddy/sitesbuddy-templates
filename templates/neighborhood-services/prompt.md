# Neighborhood Services — Template Customisation Guide

This is a single-tradesperson or small trades business template. The phone number is the primary CTA throughout. The design communicates trust, professionalism, and fast local response.

## Key things to customise

**Business identity**
- Replace "BrennanTrades" with the actual business name throughout (nav, footer, page title)
- Update the callout strip tagline (currently "Same-day call-outs available")
- Change "Pat Brennan" in the about section to the actual person's name

**Phone number**
- Replace `01 000 0000` everywhere — it appears in the callout strip, nav CTA, hero, and CTA section
- Make sure all `tel:` href values also match the new number

**Services**
- The services grid shows 6 service cards — keep only the ones that apply
- Update each card's name, description, and the job-type tags
- Add or remove cards as needed

**How it works**
- Update the 3-step process if the booking method differs (e.g. if there's an online form rather than phone)

**About section**
- Update years of experience (currently 15+)
- Replace the personal bio text with the actual tradesperson's story
- Update credentials — the 4 credential bullets (RGI, Safe Electric, insurance, reviews) should be accurate

**Coverage area**
- Replace the list of 12 area names with the actual towns and villages covered

**Reviews**
- Replace reviewer names, locations, and quotes with real reviews

**Colours**
- `--navy: #1a3050` — dark blue (nav, hero, reviews section)
- `--orange: #e55a00` — strong orange (CTAs, callout strip, hover states)

## What this template works for
- Plumber, electrician, gas installer
- Painter and decorator
- Carpenter or joiner
- Cleaning or housekeeping service
- Gardener or landscaper
- Any sole-trader or small local trades business
