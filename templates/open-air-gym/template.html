One yard, four useful zones

Train without waiting for a machine.

Come with your own plan or join a coached session. Every zone has a clear purpose, plenty of space, and equipment that can handle real weather.

01

Strength deck

Six racks, platforms, barbells, dumbbells, benches, and plates—with a full canopy overhead.

02

Engine lane

Sled track, rowers, bikes, ropes, sandbags, and enough distance for carries without weaving through people.

03

Movement lawn

Turf for mobility, kettlebells, bodyweight work, small groups, and the sessions that need room.

04

Quiet corner

A lower-noise covered area for warm-ups, rehab plans, stretching, and taking your time.

Your first visit

Walk in knowing what happens next.

Reserve a day pass, arrive ten minutes early, and meet the coach at the orange gate. We’ll show you the zones, storage, and weather setup before you train.

  • Bring training shoes, a towel, and a water bottle
  • Changing rooms and hot showers are indoors
  • Beginners are welcome during every coached-floor period
  • Heavy rain moves coached sessions beneath the canopy
