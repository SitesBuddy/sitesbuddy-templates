Common questions

FAQ

How exactly does the price lock work?
When you sign up, we record your bundle price. That price will not increase as long as your subscription is active, regardless of what happens to our underlying costs. We absorb standard market movements — you only ever pay your locked-in rate.
Can I change the items in my bundle?
Community bundle subscribers can request up to 4 item swaps per month at no charge. Starter and Family bundle holders can swap 1 item per month. Some premium substitutions may have a small additional charge.
How do I cancel?
You can cancel at any time from your account page. There are no cancellation fees, no notice period for month-to-month plans, and no retention pressure. If you cancel mid-month, we'll still deliver your current month's bundle.
What's your delivery area?
We deliver within 25km of our base in Millbrook. If you're outside that radius, enter your postcode at checkout and we'll confirm availability. We're expanding the delivery area every quarter.
