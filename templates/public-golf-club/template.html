The course at High Meadow

Open enough to enjoy. Interesting enough to return.

An 18-hole parkland course shaped around old meadow lines, mature trees, and generous walking routes. Five tee options keep the day playable for different handicaps.

Your first visit

Everything useful, in one visitor guide.

Arrive 35 minutes before your tee time. Check in at the pro shop, collect range balls, and use the practice green before walking to the first tee.

Dress code

Golf clothing is requested on the course. Smart casual is welcome in the clubhouse; clean trainers and tailored shorts are fine.

Club and trolley hire

Full sets are €28, pull trolleys €6, and electric trolleys €15. Reserve equipment with your tee time.

Practice and warm-up

Your green fee includes 25 range balls. The putting green and short-game area are beside the first tee.

Food before or after

Breakfast is served from 07:30. The terrace menu runs until 19:00, with sandwiches available for the course.

The turn and the terrace

Stay for the view, not the dress code.

Coffee before the round, a halfway sandwich, or lunch on the terrace. Visitors are welcome throughout the clubhouse from the moment the doors open.
