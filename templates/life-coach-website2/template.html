Meet your coach

Hi, I'm Mara. I help people find their next honest step.

I'm a certified life coach with a background in psychology and 5+ years of experience supporting people through career changes, burnout recovery, confidence work, and everyday decision fatigue.

Most of my clients come to me when life looks fine from the outside, but inside they feel stuck, stretched thin, or unsure what they actually want next.

Our sessions slow the noise down, name what matters, and turn big questions into small next steps you can actually live with.

Certified coach Psychology background 5+ years experience Transition support

Who this is for

For people who are ready to stop carrying every question alone.

  • You are making a career or life decision and want a clearer way to think.
  • You feel stretched thin, even when everything looks fine from the outside.
  • You want steadier habits without turning your life into a self-improvement project.
  • You need a practical next step, not another motivational speech.
Psychology-informed, future-focused coaching.

Sessions combine reflection, practical planning, and gentle accountability. The work is not therapy, and it is not a performance. It is a calm place to name what matters and choose what happens next.
