# Emergency Electrician

Build a fast, reassurance-led website for a local 24/7 residential electrician. The visitor is often stressed and needs a direct phone call, not a long lead form. Make the phone number and `Call now` action consistently obvious. Separate emergency call-outs from planned repairs and complete-home electrical projects, while giving each route a clear next action. Use a deep navy and electrical-yellow palette, practical language, local service-area reassurance, and photography that shows competent electrical work without logos or unsafe instructions.
