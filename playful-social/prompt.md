# Playful Social — Template Context

## Page Type
Bold & bubbly bio/link page — fun personality-driven design.

## Design System
- **Palette**: Indigo (#6366F1), Pink (#EC4899), Cream (#FFFBF5)
- **Fonts**: Rounded sans-serif headings, clean body text
- **Style**: Large border-radius, playful spacing, bright gradients, fun micro-interactions
- **Tone**: Energetic, approachable, personality-forward

## Section Structure
1. **Hero** — Colorful introduction with name/brand + tagline + avatar/photo area
2. **About** — Short bio with personality
3. **Links / Features** — Social links or service cards in a stacked layout
4. **Social Proof** — Fun testimonials or highlights
5. **CTA** — Follow/connect/hire me call-to-action
6. **Footer** — Minimal sign-off

## Suggested First Edits
- Add your name, photo, and personal tagline to the hero
- Replace the link cards with your actual social media and website links
- Change the indigo/pink accent to match your personal brand colors
