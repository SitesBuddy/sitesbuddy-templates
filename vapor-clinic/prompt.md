# Vapor Clinic — Template Context

## Page Type
Neon biotech sales/landing page — futuristic, electric, premium dark.

## Design System
- **Palette**: Deep Void (dark navy/black base), Plasma (violet/purple neon), Ghost (light text/accents)
- **Fonts**: Futuristic sans-serif for headings, clean sans for body
- **Style**: Neon glows, gradient effects, dark backgrounds, GSAP scroll animations
- **Tone**: Cutting-edge, clinical yet electric, Tokyo nightclub meets genome lab

## Section Structure
1. **Hero** — Deep dark backdrop + neon-glow headline + gradient CTA
2. **Problem** — Future-forward pain points
3. **Solution / Features** — 3 cards with neon borders and glow effects
4. **Social Proof** — 2–3 testimonials
5. **CTA Block** — Neon-accented final push
6. **Footer** — Brand with subtle glow

## Suggested First Edits
- Change the headline and subheading to your product pitch
- Adjust the neon purple/violet to your brand accent color
- Replace feature descriptions with your actual service offerings
